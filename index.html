Highlights

Highlights

My journey from an active participant to a community leader is a testament to my passion and commitment to fostering a vibrant tech community from organizing and speaking at events for Microsoft, Amazon Web Service, Google and KCDs by CNCF. Stay tuned for more transformative events and join the journey!

My first event as AWS Cloud Club Captain featured Stephen Howell, who provided hands-on demonstrations of Amazon Rekognition and Amazon Bedrock. The event's success was evident with 1.3K views on Facebook Live and a growing meetup group of 600+ members.

In collaboration with TensorFlow User Group Bangladesh, I facilitated a session featuring Jay Thakkar on Google's Gemma models. This event was part of the #BuildwithAI series and was well-received by the AI community.

I led a team to victory in the AWS PartyRock Hackathon, creating ModelHub - a platform for sharing pre-trained models in autonomous racing. Our project secured a bonus prize among 8,000 participants

Hosted first tech workshop on Azure Language Studio, covering ML basics and hands-on training. Demonstrated model creation, prediction, and Azure overview. Engaged audience with Q&A, sharing experiences and insights on Microsoft programs.

Another highlight was the ML Paper Reading Club session led by Dr. Aniqua Zereen, which focused on video analytics for elderly fall detection. The session attracted 2.7K views on Facebook in just two days, reflecting the community's strong engagement.

I also hosted a session on Microsoft certifications, drawing 546 registrations and 87 live attendees. The event covered exam preparation strategies and the benefits of Microsoft Learn Student Ambassador Program.
